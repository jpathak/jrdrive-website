All Your Cloud Storage in One App

Manage All Your Cloud Storage

JRDrive connects to Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, S3, and many more cloud services. Browse, stream, and manage your files from one unified interface.

Key Features

Multi-Cloud Support

Connect to dozens of cloud storage providers including Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, Amazon S3, Backblaze B2, WebDAV, and more.

Video Streaming

Stream videos directly from your cloud storage with the built-in video player. No need to download entire files first.

Encrypted Storage

Keep your files private with client-side encryption. Only you have the keys - your cloud provider cannot read your data.

Thumbnail Previews

Visual previews for images and videos make it easy to find the files you are looking for.

File Management

Upload, download, rename, move, copy, and delete files across all your cloud storage services.

Privacy Focused

Your files never pass through our servers. All connections are direct between your device and your cloud providers.
